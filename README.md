## What This Wiki Covers

The wiki documents both core and advanced aspects of Stardew Valley, including:

- Crops, farming mechanics, and seasonal planning  
- NPC schedules, gifts, and relationship mechanics  
- Skills, professions, and progression systems  
- Items, crafting recipes, and resources  
- Events, festivals, and in-game secrets  
- Modding-related references and technical notes  

All pages are structured for **quick lookup**, **clear explanations**, and **search visibility**.

## Why Another Stardew Valley Wiki?

Many Stardew Valley resources are bloated, outdated, or hard to navigate.  
This project focuses on:

- Simple layouts  
- Fast-loading pages  
- Direct answers without unnecessary fluff  
- Clean URLs and strong internal linking  

The site is built as a **reference-first wiki**, not a news blog or content farm.
